Welcome to NovaCrest Hospital

Dedicated To Healing Minds,
Restoring Hope

  • Psychiatric Assessment & Diagnosis
  • Inpatient & Outpatient Care
  • Psychological Evaluation
  • Therapy & Counseling
  • Addiction Rehabilitation

NovaCrest Hospital began operations on November 21, 2011, originally registered as PICK Specialist Hospital and Diagnostic Centre. Since then, we have impacted over 5,000 lives, serving clients across Nigeria, Africa, Europe, and North America.
As a center of excellence in Bio-Psychological Medicine, Mental Health, Neurology, and Addiction Rehabilitation, we provide first-class, personalized healthcare in a serene and exclusive environment.

Our expert team is led by a seasoned Consultant Neuro-Psychiatrist, supported by dedicated doctors, nurses, clinical psychologists, and administrative staff—all committed to compassionate, research-driven, and innovative care. At NovaCrest, we don’t just treat; we transform lives.
